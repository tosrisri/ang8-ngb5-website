UX Audit/Expert Review

Is your product not converting? With an Audit I will rapidly identify the UX flaws in your project.

What is it?

A UX Audit is a cost effective way to get fast expert advice to pinpoint crucial flaws in your digital product. The audit will address obstacles and will give you a report with clear prioritised actionable advice to help you turn your product into a positive meaningful experience that will lead to loyal users, increased conversion rates and user satisfaction.

Who is it for?
  • Startups
  • Product owners
  • Investors
  • Digital agencies
  • Anyone interested in optimising their digital product or experience
HOW DOES IT WORK?

A UX Audit uses various tools, best practises and metrics to help you too
rapidly improve your product.

Below an overview of the process:
  1. During a 30 minute discovery (video) call we will discuss the project, business requirements and user goals.
  2. I will execute the audit using a variety of tools, methods & metrics.‍
  3. During a second 30 minute (video) call we will discuss my initial findings.‍
  4. I'll create a report that includes prioritised recommendations, clear actionable advice and wireframe concepts.
  5. During our final 30 minute (video) call we will go through my report and you will have the opportunity to ask any final questions and discuss an optional follow up project.
EXAMPLE SCENARIOS
  • Improve your user onboarding process
  • Streamline your check out process
  • Optimise complex user dashboards
  • Applying UX best practises to your product
Turn around time varies between 1 week to 2 weeks depending on scope & project complexity
